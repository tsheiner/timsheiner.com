A note about this video

I teach a course in Systems in the Interaction Design Program at the California College of the Arts. This is a video documenting a collaborative project between my students and The Adobe Creative Cloud team. While it is not a sample of me giving a talk, I do appear in the video several times, both interacting with the students as well speaking directly to the camera to describe the format and goals of the project.

I hope from viewing this you will appreciate that I am an experienced public speaker and that, should you interested in the content of my talk, you can be assured that my presentation of it will be clear, engaging and effective.
